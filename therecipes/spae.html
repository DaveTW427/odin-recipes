Ingredients

  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 8 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk, or water

Instructions

  1. Whisk together flour, eggs, milk, and salt. stir until the batter is well combined and develops bubbles let sit for 5-10
  2. press batter though spaetzle maker or large holed colander into simmering water
  3. let cook for about 2-3 minutes or until they float to the top
  4. serve them immediately or saute in butter ti crisp them up a little
