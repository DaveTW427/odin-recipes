German Red Cabbage

    Ingredients

  • 1/2 large head of cabbage
  • 1 apple cut in half
  • 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4-5 whole cloves or 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions

  1. Cut the cabbage into thin slices and place half into a medium heavy size pot
  2. grate one half of the apple over the cabbage and poke cloves into other side of the apple and add to pot
  3. cover with remaining cabbage. Then add vinegar, water, sugar, salt, and pepper. stir then pour over cabbage
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of butter and cover pot, bring to a boil, reduce to simmer and cooke for 1:30-2 hours
  5. Remove apple with cloves and discard. Stir before serving
